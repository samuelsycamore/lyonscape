Our Services

Lyonscape is a client-oriented company that ensures the design, installation, and maintenance of any project will be a pleasant and comfortable process for the homeowner.

Lyonscape emphasizes quality workmanship and customer satisfaction while maintaining open communication at all times. Our primary goal is to show the utmost respect to clients and address any concerns they may have before, during and after any project.

As a full-service landscape company, we provide all landscaping needs including design, installation, maintenance, hardscape, softscape, water features and lighting for residential and commercial clients.

We are proud to serve Santa Cruz and the surrounding county.

About Lyonscape

Lyonscape is a landscape company started by Santa Cruz native, Michael Eric Lyons. Michael held lead positions for several of the top landscaping companies in the area and also worked on many of America’s top-100 golf courses, including the famed Olympic Club in San Francisco, and Pebble Beach in Carmel. Michael has a Bachelor's Degree from Rutgers University in Plant and Soil Sciences.

Throughout his years of experience and education, Michael’s focus was primarily plant and soil science, as well as water management. Michael is talented in harmonizing nature with the demands of necessity. His commitment to customer satisfaction and open communication make Lyonscape one of the leading landscape companies in Santa Cruz county.
